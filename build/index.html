Jack is a Digital Designer and Web Developer working on projects with a focus on functional Design with plenty of personality and playful interaction.

Alongside studio projects, he works on VVatch.tv and OKgrl and previously worked at Future Corp in Studio Three, and at Relax, We Are The Good Guys in Berlin.

He is a graduate of Visual Communication at the Royal College of Art, and of Digital Media at Hyper Island (Stockholm).
